# Product Roadmap

# ThreadVault
> This roadmap outlines the phased evolution of ThreadVault, prioritizing core wardrobe visibility first, followed by outfit planning and intelligent social features.

## Phase 1: MVP Foundation (v1 — Shipped)
**Goal:** Validate core wardrobe management value
- Digital wardrobe catalog (item upload, edit, delete)
- Custom categories and category-based browsing
- Visual wardrobe overview and item counts
- Defined success metrics and self-testing

**Rationale:** Focused on establishing a reliable, low-friction foundation before expanding functionality.

## Phase 2: Structured Outfit & Sharing Expansion
**Goal:** Support outfit planning and lightweight collaboration
- Outfit creation by grouping clothing items
- Event-based organization (e.g., creating outfits for specific occasions)
- Outfit of the Day (OOTD) tracking
- Viewing past outfits to reduce repetition
- Sharing individual items, categories, or outfits in view-only mode
- Improved browsing and filtering for outfit planning

**Rationale:** Builds on an existing wardrobe catalog to reduce planning friction without heavy automation.

## Phase 3: Intelligent & Social Experiences
**Goal:** Enhance decision-making through intelligence and collaboration
- AI-assisted outfit suggestions and pairing insights
- Weather-aware outfit recommendations
- Advanced sharing and social interactions (e.g., friends voting on outfits)
- Inspiration and discovery through creators or references

**Rationale:** Introduced only after validating core usage and user engagement.
